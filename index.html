Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the personal automation workflows and application integrations managed by Romy Macasieb. This application is utilized strictly for personal productivity and automated workflow integrations with Google Workspace.

1. Data Collection and Use

Our application requests access to specific Google Workspace user data solely to execute automated user-directed actions and productivity scripts. We do not transfer, sell, or share any user data with third parties. All data processed remains strictly within the automated workspace environment.

2. Data Retention and Storage

No user data is stored permanently on external servers. Data processed during workflow execution is held temporarily in volatile memory only for the duration required to complete the specific automated task.

The application's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

For questions or inquiries regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the administrator directly at your designated Google Workspace email account.
